NOTE: system_profiler reports "S.M.A.R.T. status: Verified" but this is
misleading. diskutil reports "SMART Status: Not Supported" and smartctl
cannot open the device. The system_profiler value likely reflects the
enclosure's firmware reporting healthy to the USB layer, NOT actual SMART
data from the drive platters. This is a known macOS discrepancy.
